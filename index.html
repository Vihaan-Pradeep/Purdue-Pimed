Our Purpose

Who we are &
why we exist

PiMed was founded on the belief that every aspiring healthcare professional deserves access to mentorship, community, and real-world experience.

Mission Statement
"To connect medical and healthcare professionals with students to foster networking, knowledge-sharing, and career advancement opportunities — while building an inclusive community that serves Indianapolis."

Purdue in Indianapolis Medical Association · Est. 2022

Our Pillars

Four commitments that
guide everything we do

01

Education

We create spaces where students can learn from professionals and each other — in classrooms, labs, clinics, and everywhere between.

02

Inclusivity

Medicine is for everyone. We actively work to ensure PiMed is welcoming, diverse, and accessible to all students regardless of background.

03

Service

We give back. Through blood drives, volunteering, and community outreach, we put our values into action in the Indianapolis community.

04

Innovation

At Purdue, we sit at the intersection of engineering and medicine. We embrace new ideas and cross-disciplinary collaboration.
